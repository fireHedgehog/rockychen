About Me

Hi, I'm Rujia Chen, a Ph.D. candidate specializing in computer science and software engineering. My research delves into cutting-edge technologies, leveraging artificial intelligence, machine learning, and audio signal processing to explore innovative solutions in musical instrument recognition and beyond.

With a background in Computer Vision and AI, I work on developing advanced models that bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications. My passion lies in understanding complex patterns in data and using these insights to drive impactful innovations.

Research Focus

My primary research interests include:

  • Deep learning algorithms for musical instrument recognition
  • Semi-supervised and unsupervised learning for ecological data analysis
  • AI-driven spectrogram analysis for audio classification
  • Applications of Computer Vision in audio-visual synchronization
  • Feature extraction and optimization in audio signal processing
  • Exploring multi-modal data fusion for enhanced prediction accuracy
